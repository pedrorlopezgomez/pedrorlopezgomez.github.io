About

Scholar · arXiv · ORCID · MathSciNet

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at CUNEF Universidad. I earned my PhD in Mathematics from Universidad de Cantabria in 2025, supervised by Carlos Beltrán and Ujué Etayo, and I am a member of the TEAMCO group.

My research is focused on the classical question of equidistributing points on manifolds and related problems.

You can reach me by email at pedro.lopezgomez@cunef.edu.
